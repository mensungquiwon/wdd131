Country: Liberia

Liberia, located on the west coast of Africa, is known for its rich history and diverse culture. As a country founded by freed American slaves in the 19th century, Liberia boasts a unique blend of African and American influences. The nation is characterized by its lush landscapes, vibrant traditions, and resilient people who continue to work towards growth and development. The settlers, known as Americo-Liberians, brought with them American customs, political systems, and Christianity. Despite being a small minority, they dominated the political and economic life of the country for over a century. Liberia declared its independence on July 26, 1847, becoming Africa’s first republic and one of only two African countries (alongside Ethiopia) that were never colonized by European powers during the Scramble for Africa.
